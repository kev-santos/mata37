How tutorials will run

  • Starting in Week 3 (week of May 23rd), tutorials will have an evaluation every week.
  • Evaluations will be held at the start of tutorial.
  • The evaluation will be based on the assignment released the previous week. If this is week N, it will be based on assignment N-2.
  • This evaluation will be either a quiz OR you will hand in your solutions to the homework questions from the relevant assignment. (Only the "Homework" questions, not the "Exercises")
  • You will find out which it will be (quiz or hand-in) at the start of tutorial.
  • Before tutorial, you should have completed and written down your solutions to the homework questions.
  • You may only submit evaluations in the tutorial you're officially registered in (the one you're enrolled in on ACORN).

Some advice for this course

  • Make your own set of notes so you can easily refer to definitions and theorems.
  • Do all the questions and exercises on the assignment, not just the ones you have to hand in.
  • Practice the T/F questions in particular.
  • Attend office hours and ask questions.
